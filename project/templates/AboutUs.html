About Us

Our aim as an organization is to provide versatile and cost-effective software. We have come up with the idea "People with similar passion or pastime activity can all come together onto a common platform".
Based on this idea we have designed a web-application "Catch-Up" where thousands of university students with varied interests can all collaborate and connect with each other.
